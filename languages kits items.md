## II. Kits

Kits define a martial hero's equipment, preferred fighting style, and grant special mechanical benefits, including **Kit Bonuses** and a **Signature Ability**. Heroes generally cannot gain benefits from armor or weapons outside of their chosen kit's equipment type.

### Kit Components and Bonuses

| Component | Description/Bonuses |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Equipment** | Defines armor (None, Light, Medium, Heavy, Shield) and weapons (Light, Medium, Heavy, Bow, Polearm, Whip, etc.). |
| **Stamina Bonus** | Added to maximum Stamina, scales per Echelon (+3, +6, +9, or +12). |
| **Damage Bonuses** | Increases damage rolls (+X/+Y/+Z for Tier 1/2/3 results) for specific weapon types. |
| **Distance Bonus** | Increases melee or ranged weapon distance. |
| **Signature Ability** | A unique ability usable without spending a Heroic Resource. |

### Sample Kits

| Kit Name | Equipment Focus | Key Benefit Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shining Armor** | Heavy Armor, Shield, Medium Weapon | Highest Stamina Bonus (+12 per echelon), Taunts Foes |
| **Mountain** | Heavy Armor, Heavy Weapon | High Stability (+2), High Tier 3 Damage (+0/+0/+4) |
| **Swashbuckler** | Light Armor, Medium Weapon | High Speed (+3), Melee Damage (+2/+2/+2), Mobility |
| **Sniper** | None, Bow | High Ranged Distance (+10), Tier 3 Ranged Damage (+0/+0/+4) |
| **Martial Artist** | None, Unarmed Strikes | High Speed (+3), Feinting and Positioning |

## III. Downtime Projects

Downtime Projects are tasks undertaken during a **Respite**. Progress is earned through **Project Rolls** (a specialized test). Projects require a **Project Goal** (total points needed) and **Project Prerequisites** (Item and/or Project Source).

### Project Types and Goals

| Project Name | Type | Project Goal (Base/Obscure/Lost/Forbidden) | Key Outcome/Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Discover Lore** | Research | Varies (15 / 45 / 120 / 240) | Uncovers specific lore about subjects (e.g., history, strategy, monsters). |
| **Craft Treasure** | Crafting | Varies (e.g., 150 for 1st-level enhancement) | Creates supernatural armor, weapons, implements, or consumables. |
| **Find a Cure** | Research | 50 × creature level | Creates an antidote to curses/diseases/afflictions. |
| **Perfect New Recipe** | Crafting | 100 | Creates food that grants temporary beneficial effects (e.g., Hearty, Supernatural Power). |
| **Learn New Language** | Research | 120 | Hero learns a new language. |
| **Hone Career Skills** | Research | 240 or 360 | Grants an edge on tests using career skills. |
| **Community Service** | Other | 75 | Earns Renown or consumable treasure from the local community. |
| **Spend Time with Loved Ones** | Other | 60 | Increases hero's maximum Stamina until next respite. |

### Imbue Treasure Enhancements (Craft Treasure)

Leveled Treasures (Armor, Implement, Weapon) gain **Enhancements** at 1st, 5th, and 9th levels. Enhancements provide powerful features that grow stronger with the hero.

| Enhancement Level | Sample Armor | Sample Implement | Sample Weapon |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1st** | Damage Immunity I, Phasing I (Move through 1 sq. of matter), Magic Resistance I | Berserking (Extra attacks on Tier 3), Elemental (Attune to an element), Warding I (Stamina boost) | Chilling I (Cold damage), Terrifying I (Psychic damage), Hurling (Melee weapon gains ranged distance) |
| **5th** | Absorption (Absorbs enemy abilities), Dragon Soul (Damage on winded), Phasing II (Move through 3 sq. of matter) | Celestine (Conjures exploding stars), Hallucinatory (Area effect illusions), Forceful II (Increased push/pull) | Devastating (Improved Critical Hit range), Silencing (Negates magic ability use), Vengeance II (Extra damage vs. recent damage dealers) |
| **9th** | Invulnerable (Ignore Tier 1 damage/effects), Leyline Walker (Teleports as movement), Magic Resistance III (Share resistance with allies) | Anathema (Inflicts weakened), Erupting II (Increased explosion damage), Warding III (Increased resistance bonus for allies) | Imprisoning (Restrains enemy), Nova (Area explosion), Windcutting (Area attacks with melee weapons) |

## IV. Treasures (Items)

Treasures are supernatural items found, earned, or crafted by heroes.

### Consumables

These are temporary treasures expended after one use or after their charges run out.

| Echelon | Example Item | Key Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1st** | **Healing Potion** | Regain Stamina equal to Recovery Value without spending a Recovery. |
| **1st** | **Catapult Dust** | Throws targets in a fixed area 6 + 1d6 squares horizontally. |
| **2nd** | **Scroll of Resurrection** | Revives a willing creature who died within 24 hours (consumed upon use). |
| **2nd** | **Purified Jelly** | Grants the ability to breathe water and ignore inhaled poisons for 1 hour. |
| **3rd** | **Stygian Liquor** | Grants tenacious will to cling to life (don't die until Stamina hits negative maximum). |
| **4th** | **Elixir of Saint Elspeth** | Enemy abilities targeting user automatically obtain Tier 1 outcome for several rounds. |

### Trinkets

These treasures offer minor, persistent benefits and are tiered by Echelon.

| Echelon | Example Item | Key Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1st** | **Color Cloak (Red)** | Grants Fire Immunity equal to hero Level (and temporary Fire Weakness if used defensively). |
| **2nd** | **Mediator’s Charm** | Increases NPC patience in negotiation and reveals initial motivation/pitfall. |
| **3rd** | **Mask of Oversight** | Grants 360-degree vision and automatically reveals hidden creatures. |
| **4th** | **Hagbasket** | Acts as a size 2 flying mount that grants defensive bonuses to the rider. |

### Leveled Treasures

Weapons, Armor, and Implements that gain power at 1st, 5th, and 9th levels via **Enhancements**. Heroes can only safely carry three Leveled Treasures at a time.

| Type | Example Treasure | Echelon | Key 1st-Level Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Armor** | **Kuran’zoi Prismscale** | 1st | Grants Stamina bonus; on taking damage, slows attacker (Time Raider origin). |
| **Weapon** | **Displacer (Axe)** | 1st | Deals psychic damage; on rolled damage, can teleport user and target to swap places. |
| **Implement** | **Sanctuary Horn** | 1st | Magic/Psionic strikes deal Sonic damage; maneuver to teleport ally to user. |

### Artifacts

Artifacts are unique, campaign-defining items with enormous, often dangerous, power.

| Artifact Name | Type | Key Effect/Pact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blade of a Thousand Years** | Weapon | Takes desired weapon form; deals Holy damage; grants massive combat buffs to wielder and allies, but risks martyred soul on failure. |
| **Mortal Coil** | Psionic | Host gains extra action but ages rapidly and can never heal; projects a 10-mile field that prevents targets from healing/regaining stamina and gives mortals the power to kill immortals. |
| **Encepter** | Magic | Wielder gains immense Presence focus; can lasso creatures with light and destroy lassoed enemies in a burst of light (Obliteration). |
