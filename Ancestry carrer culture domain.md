# Draw Steel Character Construction Compendium

This document details the mechanical and narrative features provided during the construction of a *Draw Steel* character, covering Ancestries, Cultures, Careers, and Divine Domains.

***

## I. Ancestries

Ancestry defines a humanoid creature’s species and grants inherent physiological benefits and traits. Base Size is 1M (Medium), Speed is 5, and Stability is 0, unless otherwise noted.

## II. Cultures

Culture grants three skills (one from each aspect below) and core social benefits.

**Core Benefits:** Know the language of the culture (plus Caelian); gain **one skill** from each of the three aspects below (Environment, Organization, Upbringing); gain an **edge** on tests made to recall lore about and interact with people of that culture.

| Aspect | Description | Skill Options |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Where the community spends most of their time. | **Nomadic:** Exploration or Interpersonal. **Rural:** Crafting or Lore. **Secluded:** Interpersonal or Lore. **Urban:** Interpersonal or Intrigue. **Wilderness:** Crafting or Exploration. |
| **Organization** | The leadership and functioning of the community. | **Bureaucratic:** Interpersonal or Intrigue. **Communal:** Crafting or Exploration. |
| **Upbringing** | How the hero was raised within their culture. | **Academic:** Lore. **Creative:** Music/Perform (Interpersonal) or Crafting. **Labor:** Blacksmithing (Crafting), Handle Animals (Interpersonal), or Exploration. **Lawless:** Intrigue. **Martial:** Blacksmithing/Fletching (Crafting), Climb/Endurance/Ride (Exploration), Intimidate (Interpersonal), Alertness/Track (Intrigue), or Monsters/Strategy (Lore). **Noble:** Interpersonal. |
